# Fit Scoring Rubric

## Goal

The 100-point rubric makes candidate-to-role matching more consistent. It is not
intended to predict hiring outcomes with false precision.

## Score Breakdown

| Dimension | Points | Meaning |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Hard requirements | 30 | Degree, years of experience, required skills, location, work eligibility. |
| Experience relevance | 40 | Industry, function, project similarity, AI/product exposure, business impact. |
| Soft fit and growth | 30 | Company stage, role growth, learning curve, team context, candidate preference. |

## Bands

| Score | Recommendation |
| ---: | --- |
| 90-100 | Highly recommended. Strong fit worth prioritizing. |
| 75-89 | Recommended. Good fit with manageable gaps. |
| 60-74 | Possible. Apply selectively or after targeted optimization. |
| Below 60 | Lower priority. Explain the main mismatch. |

## Hard Requirements: 30 Points

Suggested allocation:

- education and credential fit: 8
- years or seniority fit: 6
- required skills: 10
- location and availability: 4
- language or domain requirements: 2

Hard requirements should not be over-optimized with invented experience.

## Experience Relevance: 40 Points

Suggested allocation:

- similar product or role experience: 12
- AI, data, or technical collaboration experience: 10
- project ownership and impact: 8
- industry or user-domain relevance: 5
- portfolio, awards, writing, or public proof: 5

## Soft Fit And Growth: 30 Points

Suggested allocation:

- company stage and candidate growth match: 8
- team structure and mentorship likelihood: 6
- role scope and learning opportunity: 6
- culture and work style match: 5
- candidate interest and motivation: 5

## Output Standard

Every score should include:

- the score,
- two to three evidence points,
- key gaps,
- mitigation suggestions.

## Interview Talking Point

The rubric is useful not because every point is mathematically exact, but because
it forces the model to separate hard fit, experience fit, and growth fit instead
of saying "this company feels suitable".
