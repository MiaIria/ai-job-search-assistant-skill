# Company Research Framework

## Goal

Company research should help the candidate decide whether an opportunity is
worth applying to and how to prepare.

The report should include upside and risk. Do not write company marketing copy.

## Five Research Dimensions

### 1. Company Basics And Business Strength

Research:

- legal or official company name,
- founding time and headquarters,
- main product or service,
- business model,
- funding or listing status,
- recent business changes,
- market position.

Prefer official websites, filings, credible news, and investor materials.

### 2. Culture And Values

Research:

- official values,
- management style,
- team atmosphere,
- employee growth signals,
- innovation tolerance,
- pressure level.

Use employee review platforms carefully. Treat them as signals, not facts.

### 3. Working Model

Research:

- working hours,
- remote or hybrid policy,
- overtime pattern,
- attendance system,
- probation period,
- performance review cycle.

Label weakly sourced claims clearly.

### 4. Compensation And Benefits

Research:

- salary range if available,
- salary structure,
- bonus and year-end patterns,
- social insurance and housing fund,
- paid leave,
- meal, commute, training, health, or stock benefits.

Do not invent salary or benefits when unavailable.

### 5. AI Team Context

Research:

- AI product lines,
- team size if public,
- PM collaboration with algorithm or engineering teams,
- tech stack signals,
- mentor or growth path,
- role scope for the target position.

This dimension is especially important for AI product manager applications.

## Red Flag Examples

- stale listings,
- unclear employer identity,
- outsourcing disguised as product role,
- salary range inconsistent across sources,
- employee reviews repeatedly mentioning unpaid overtime,
- role title does not match actual responsibilities.

## Interview Talking Point

The company research framework exists because candidates do not only need "who is
hiring". They need to know which opportunities are worth spending interview and
resume-customization time on.
